How It Works

  • Pay 21 sats via Lightning to play.
  • ~76.2% (16 sats) funds the Jackpot Pool.
  • 3 cards are drawn, revealing your fortune.
  • Wins add to your 'Balance' below.
  • WIN TIERS (Pool Permitting):
    - JACKPOT! (Sun+World+Magician): Wins ENTIRE pool!
    - Major Win: ~35% pool.
    - Minor Win: ~15% pool.
  • WARNING: Jackpot is LIVE & shared! Amount can change instantly.
  • WITHDRAW: Click 'Withdraw Sats' (if balance > 0) to get a LNURL withdrawal QR code.

How To Verify

  1. Click the Verify tab to get your Block Hash and Seed.
  2. Check the block hash at mempool.space
  3. Run this in your terminal to confirm the seed:
Show verification code

const crypto = require('crypto');

  .digest('hex');
console.log('Seed:', parseInt(seed.substring(0,8), 16));"
